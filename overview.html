Overview

Welcome to the Mental Health Toolkit. In this overview, you'll find an introduction to the resources designed to improve mental well‑being in the workplace. Our toolkit provides comprehensive training materials, practical tools, and expert insights to help organizations create supportive and resilient work environments.

We cover a range of topics including mindfulness practices, effective communication, stress management techniques, and strategies to combat stigma around mental health. Whether you’re an HR professional, manager, or employee, our goal is to empower you with the knowledge and skills needed to foster mental wellness at work.
