Executive Summary

Mental health is a foundational component of workplace success. However, stigma, lack of awareness, and inadequate resources often prevent employees from receiving the support they need. This project was developed to provide organizations with a practical, accessible toolkit to address these challenges.

Our Mental Health in the Workplace Training includes a variety of deliverables—from slide decks and podcasts to infographics and assessments—each designed to promote psychological safety, increase mental health literacy, and provide actionable strategies for employee well-being.

Whether you're an HR professional, manager, or team member, this toolkit is meant to help you create a more supportive, informed, and stigma-free work environment.
